# AI-Generated AGI Architecture Proposals

## Overview
This research packet contains AGI architecture proposals generated by multiple AI systems. The goal is to compare and analyze these proposals to inform the planning of Cognitive-OS.

## Collection Method
- **Prompt**: A consistent prompt was used across all AI systems, with minor adaptations as needed.
- **AI Systems**: At least 8 distinct AI systems or model families were used.
- **Documentation**: Each proposal is documented in the `raw_outputs/` directory, and the prompts are recorded in `prompts.md`.

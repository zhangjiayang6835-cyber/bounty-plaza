# Bounty Plaza Usage Guide

## Creating a Bounty
1. Navigate to the bounty creation form
2. Fill in all required fields:
   - Title
   - Description
   - Reward amount
   - Difficulty level
3. Submit the form

## Claiming a Bounty
1. Browse available bounties
2. Click on a bounty to view details
3. Click the "Claim Bounty" button
4. Confirm your claim

## Completing a Bounty
1. After claiming a bounty, complete the required work
2. Submit your solution through the platform
3. Wait for review and approval

## Bounty Statuses
- **Open**: Available for claiming
- **Claimed**: Currently being worked on
- **Completed**: Successfully resolved
- **Expired**: No longer available
